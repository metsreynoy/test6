Illustration refers to the visual representation or interpretation of a concept, idea, story, or subject through the use of images, drawings, paintings, or other graphic elements. Illustrations are often created to enhance, explain, or accompany written content and can be found in various forms of media, including books, magazines, advertisements, websites, and digital platforms.

CorelDRAW is a vector graphics editor and design software developed by the Canadian software company Corel Corporation. It is part of the CorelDRAW Graphics Suite, which includes additional tools for photo editing (Corel PHOTO-PAINT), font management, and other design-related functionalities. CorelDRAW is widely used by graphic designers, illustrators, and professionals in various industries for creating vector-based designs.
